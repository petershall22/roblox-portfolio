Site-NIKOLAI

Site-NIKOLAI was a project where I served as Lead Developer. It’s a sprawling underground city—a self-contained world where every individual plays a role in the massive machine that is Nikolai. Life within the facility is all-encompassing; once you enter, you live there.

From black market trading and food production to quiet resistance against the ruling powers—the Foundation—every layer of society contributes to the story. The world of Nikolai is rich with depth, conflict, and purpose, and I was responsible for bringing its systems and structure to life through immersive gameplay and technical design.
