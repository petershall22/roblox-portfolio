City-17

City-17 was a project that I developed for (made UIs and scripted them) which was based on the Half-Life 2 universe. The game was set in a dystopian world where players had to navigate through a city controlled by an oppressive regime. The game featured a variety of gameplay mechanics, including puzzle-solving, combat, and exploration.

Unfortunately, I left the project due to time constraints as well as being heavily underpaid for the work I was putting in.
