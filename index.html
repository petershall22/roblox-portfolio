About me

I'm a multi-stack developer with experience across a wide range of programming languages and tools. I specialize in crafting efficient, scalable systems using Luau, Rust, C#, Python, and Dart (primarily with the Flutter framework). My approach combines technical precision with a strong sense of design and usability.

I’ve worked extensively with development tools like Rojo, Roact, and Knit, and I’m well-versed in collaborative platforms such as GitHub, Jira, Trello, Slack, and Google Workspace. Whether it's scripting, UI programming, or full-scale system architecture, I bring a versatile and detail-oriented mindset to every project.

Previously, I served as the Lead Developer for Nikolai, where I led technical direction and development strategies. My background as a UI designer, programmer, and scripter allows me to bridge the gap between design and functionality, ensuring that every product I work on is both polished and powerful.
